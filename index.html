Our goal

We want to raise awareness about the rising issues of climate change by informing people of the main cause; Carbon emissions. CO2 accounts for 76% of greenhouse gas emissions, with the main producers of these being China, India, Europe, and the United States. 33,621 million tons of CO2 were generated worldwide in 2019. In 2013, Transportation made up 15% of carbon emissions, which bumped up to 23% in 2014. Climate change is a troubling and impactful occurrence that needs to be well-known by the world. Moving forward, we must be mindful of our production of gasses and do our best to play a role in reducing our carbon footprint as much as possible.
